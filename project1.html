Cable Testing

As the main project, I was tasked with ensuring that control panel LEDs were fully off when intended. I tested various cable configurations to design a noise‑reducing cable. The process involved building and modifying cables to isolate variables, capturing oscilloscope readings of voltage across LED control panel resistors, and compiling the results into a comprehensive presentation comparing LED brightness, peak‑to‑peak voltage, and estimated on voltage for each configuration.
