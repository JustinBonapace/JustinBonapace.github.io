ECG Signal Enhancement

This school project involved measuring ECG signals from different points on the body and enhancing the clarity of the signal. I filtered the ECG signals using both analog and digital techniques, carefully balancing gain and cutoff frequencies in cascaded filters. The data was exported to MATLAB, where I applied a digital Butterworth filter and compared the results to the analog filtering, optimizing parameters for clear and reliable ECG signal processing.
