Robotic Arm

The Robotic Arm project aimed to automate the movement of a water bottle between two user‑defined positions. Utilizing a DE1-SoC board and Quartus Prime software, I developed a system that controlled a robotic arm equipped with five servos (claw, wrist, elbow, bicep, and base). The project implemented PWM control for precise servo movements and combined both manual control and automated cycles, providing valuable experience in embedded design and real‑time automation.
